Tourism Logo Design

An immersive logo design for the
"land of the beautiful horses"- Cappadocia, Turkey.

Imagine being inside a fairy chimney and looking out into the distance-
the orange sun peeking through the other fairy chimneys afar,
the light beaming through the carved out chiseled opening from where you stood.
It was important to embody "Cappadocia" in its literal meaning, even more so to emulate the vision and warmth. As if you are able to experience a glimpse of what it would be like to be there.

About

This project was created as part of a school project. The course was ART 127 Graphic Symbolism and the assignment was to create a logo for an international city that embodies its spirit, arts, culture, history and present.
