David Sedaris Book Trilogy

Fun and quirky Illustrations for a comedic author

David Sedaris writes about personal experiences- his highest highs, his lowest lows, and everything in-between. These bookcover designs are inspired by David's natural ability to see the shiny flip-side of any situation. The aim with these illustrations is to encourage readers to shift  perspective.

Client: David Sedaris
Service: Trilogy Book Cover Designs
Skills: Illustration

Journey Into The Night

David Sedaris is on an overnight flight from New York to Paris with a seat in first-class, aka Business Elite. A seemingly pleasant and confortable flight soon turns a bit unsettling when a grieving Polish man sits next to him. This triggers David into a rollercoaster of intrusive thoughts and recollection. Snapping back to reality in Business Elite, David is humbled by witnessing all forms of life in a mere  airplane.

My Failed Attempts To Hoard Anything At All

At the start of Corona Virus pandemic- hoarding seems to come as second nature for most, except David Sedaris. Recalling the oil crisis in 1973, David was inspired by his father's "professional shopping" abilities and wanted to do the same this time around. After each grocery trip, David comes home with just a handful of random items.

Grandfather Time

In a more serious tone passage- David reflects about life and time. He is in town and visits his father in his retirement home after he had a few health episodes. It was the time of the year when baby loddgerheads hatch. Coincidentally, the very last lone loggerhead to hatch was named "Lou" just like David's  father.
