Corporate Logo Design

A sophisticated storytelling logo design for the
well established Waikiki International Marketplace

Fun fact: Waikiki translates to "spoouting water" which refers to the rich springs and rivers that used to flow through the area.
Inspired by the infamous banyan tree and the original treehouse that stood since humble beginings. This design takes a purposeful play with line work meant to compliment the distinct designs in its exsisting architecture. It also emulates the movement of flowing water.
.

Client: Waikiki International Marketplace
Service: Logo Redesign
Skills: Illustration

About

This project was created as part of a school project. The course was ART 127 Graphic Symbolism and the assignment was to create a distinct, memorable, scalable, adaptable, and appropriate logo for a company of my choice.
