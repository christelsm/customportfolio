Hi there, thanks for stopping by!

My passion for art from a young age later evolved to a passion for graphic design. During my teen years I would easily spend hours behind the computer screen editing Myspace profiles for my family and friends. It was my first experience with HTML. Just being able to transform text with a "foreign alien language" intrigued me and I would be lost in just the simplest HTML. Graphic Design stuck with me all throughout my highschool years. A "chill" class soon became my escape, plus I got to make stickers. Upon recieving my high school diploma, I also recieved a certicicate for completing the Graphic Design Technology Program at my high school. Life's rollercoaster went full force and circled me back to my passion. I am now an Interface Design Student in the New Media Arts Program at Kapiolani Community College. If im going to be riding a rollercoaster, it might as well be a rollercoaster I enjoy and love.
