Website UI/UX

Creating a website for a small resin craft business

Naturesin is a small resin craft business that creates life-time lasting peices using a harmonious blend of nature and resin. The aim with the Naturesin website was to create a minimalistic, sophisticated, and a "light/airy" vehicle to showcase the products. .

Client: Naturesin
Service: Establish Website, Web Design
Skills: Branding, UX, UI
Site: Naturesin

Concept 01

This is the UI concept that I ended up choosing. It is minimalistic, sophisticated, and "airy". This design covers all bases and its simplicity does not take away from the products allowing them to shine.


Concept 02

A rainforest feel, I felt it was appropriate for Naturesin. In this case it was nature + rain. The rain resembles resin in its purest form. I wanted to create a "tasteful" design embracing that rain forest concept while maintaining its sophisticated and minimalistic aspects. A darker color scheme also allows the images to stand out.


Concept 03

This design was the most enjoyable for me to create. I wanted to try out a more fun and abstract aesthetic without sacrificing the sophisticated attribute.

About

This project was created as part of a school project. The courses involved was ART 229 Interface Design 1 and ART 128 Interface Programming 1 and the assignment was to design and create a functioning coded website from scratch.
