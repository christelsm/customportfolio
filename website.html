Branding

Imagine being inside a fairy chimney and looking out into the distance with the orange sun peeking through the other fairy chimneys in the distance and the light beaming through the carved out chiseled opening from where you stood. It was important to embody "Cappadocia" in its true meaning. It was also just at important to emulate that vision and warmth into my design. .

Client: Naturesin
Service: Establish Website, Web Design
Skills: Branding, UX, UI

About

This project was created as part of a school project. The course was ART 127 Graphic Symbolism and the assignment was to create a distinct, memorable, scalable, adaptable, and appropriate logo for a company of my  choice.
